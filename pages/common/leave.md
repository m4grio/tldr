# leave

> Remind you when you have to leave.
> To remove reminders use `kill $(pidof leave)`.

- Set a reminder:

`leave {{time_to_leave}}`

- Remind you to leave at noon:

`leave 1200`

- Set a reminder in a specific amount of time:

`leave +{{amount_of_time}}`

- Remind you in 4 hours and 4 minutes:

`leave +0404`
